Insulated steel pipes for heating

SIA "Poliurs" produces insulated pipes for heat supply according to European Union standards: LVS EN 253. District heating pipes. Industrial insulated and sheathed piping systems for ductless hot water networks. Finished steel water pipes with polyurethane thermal insulation and polyethylene outer jacket.

Insulated fittings

SIA "Poliurs" offers all kinds of standard fittings required for the construction of heating mains, the full assortment of which is reflected in the company's product catalog . At the customer's request, non-standard fittings are made to provide the most optimal solution for the construction of insulated pipelines.

Components

In order for the insulated pipelines to be fully operational for the maximum possible service life, it is essential to ensure high-quality insulation of the pipe joints. SIA "Poliurs" offers different types of connection couplings depending on the diameter of the pipes, the required degree of sealing and the customer's wishes: PE couplings, heat-shrinkable, double-insulated and electrically weldable, as well as end couplings.

The insulated pipes and fittings manufactured by the company are equipped with a monitoring alarm wiring system, which allows to continuously monitor the technical condition of the pipelines and to detect the appearance of moisture in the thermal insulation layer in the event of a leak. Special equipment is offered for continuous control of the monitoring alarm.

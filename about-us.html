About Us

Explore the future with us. Feel free to get in touch.

Gulf Team is an ISO 9001: 2015 QMS Certified company, specializing in Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Fabrication Works. The company has grown to become one of the specialized industrial Service Maintenance and Repair company in the state of Qatar. With trained, certified and experienced Engineers and Technicians we have the capacity and expertise that is needed to meet the client's every service requirement on time and on budget. We are committed to Safety, Superior Quality, Prompt Service and Protection of Environment.

Electrical Machines Repair Shop caters to the need of Repairs, Rewinds, Redesigns, Overhauls, Laser Alignment of Submersible pumps, Site overhauling, Site bearing Replacement, Control Panel Checking Conversions and Insulation renewal of all class. Reliable and very efficient breakdown services for any make, type and size of Low, Medium and High Voltage A.C. Motors up to 3.3 kv Motors, Generators – Alternator rewinding, Engine repair and overhauling.

We are specialists in:

Electrical Works

  • Repair and Rewinding of Submersible Pumps,
  • Rewinding of Three Phase Induction Motors & Generators,
  • Rewinding of Alternator,
  • Repair and Rewiring of Control Panel,
  • Supply of MCB, ACB, MCCB, VFD panels.

Mechanical

  • All kinds of Welding works that include 6G ,4G, Aluminum, ARC, MIG, TIG,
  • Machining, Drilling, Milling of subjected material,
  • Supply & Refurbishment of Valves.

Trading

  • Supply of all kind of bearings – SKF, NSK, FAG, TIMKIN etc.
  • Supply of Mechanical seals for pumps.
  • Supply of HARDOX Plates.
  • Supply of Centrifugal & Submersible Pumps
  • Supply of and certified Valves - Gate ,NRV & Butterfly Valves.

Civil Works

  • Construction of Wall, Slab,
  • Supply and Installation of Interlocking.

Fabrication

  • Fabrication of Cast Iron,
  • Moulding Mild Steel and Stainless steel.
